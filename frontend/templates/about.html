About Eleanora

A home studio, not a factory

Eleanora Nails began at a kitchen table in {{ salon.area }} in 2021, with one lamp, one set of brushes and a lot of patience. It is still a home studio today, and that is on purpose.

Only one client is booked at a time. Nobody is waiting behind you, so nothing is rushed: the prep is done properly, the shape is filed until it is right, and the art is painted while you sit and talk. It is why a set from here lasts, and why most people who come once come back.
